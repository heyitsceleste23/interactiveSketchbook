Sketch 1: Rushed

The adjective for this sketch is rushed, because I had to leave my apartment to catch my bus for school in a hurry.
The input I used for the sketch is the background in the setup function, so the circle made would build up over time, articulating a similar feeling of that morning.

The thing that changes in my code is the circle accumulating as you move the mouse to express that your mind is racing when you feel rushed or anxious.
