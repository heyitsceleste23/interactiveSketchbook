Navigation Model & System Coherence - (20/20 pts)

I believe my navigation system is simple but works and meets all the requirements.

Hierarchy & Mobile-First Logic - 20/20 points

I believe that the desktop page was expanded on after the creation of the mobile page, both are legible and have accurate structure. layout has developed over time based on feedback.

Explain how hierarchy survives compression and expands across contexts.

The home page has a clear hierarchy in a way that the buttons are clear, spaced out well, and all lead to pages with a layout that would be straightforward enough for a user to use. The design is simple, but effort was put in to make it legible for both mobile and desktop versions. my favorite part is the home button icon, since it stays in the same place regardless of what device you're on.

Theoretical Integration (Norman / Nielsen / Shneiderman) - (8/15 pts)

I believe my design has the frameworks are referenced but not fully embodied in structure.

Cross-Page Consistency - (10/10 pts)

I believe that I deserve full points on the design of all my pages because it's consistent, clear, user friendly and clean. all the pages have a clear state, and can be referenced to each other if neccessary.

Risk & Structural Ambition (5/5 pts)

I believe that I deserve full marks on the structural ambition because I tried multiple designs, for example, the navigation bar, and didn't like the way it looked, so I tweaked it into something I could call my own, which was the little home button as a navigation system instead of a whole bar.

Iterative Development (10/10 pts)

I believe that I deserve full credit on the evolution of my system because the design has changed over time, and only stayed consistent at the very end. I made intentional changes to make the pages more legible and user friendly when it was neccessary. For example, I added a home button instead of having a bar at the top with a navigation pop up because it felt too cluttered to me, and having each week as a different page with a home button was easier to navigate than a whole navigation bar on mobile, which was part of my original design.

Participation & Critique Engagement (5/5 pts)

I believe I implemented both inspiration and motivation to work on my project after group critiques, and always enjoyed having a conversation with my classmates. I always made sure to give feedback, as well as being open for new ideas on how to improve, and implemented them in my design after the critiques.
