micro ideas

1. Confusion

The input is the question marks that show up on the screen when the mouse is moved around, and the change over time is the mouse moving.

The unevenness appears after the mouse is clicked, making the question marks disappear as the mouse is clicked.

2. Growth

The input is the growth of the solid as the mouse is clicked, and the change over time are the two bars growing with the background as the mouse is clicked.


The unevenness would appear because every other mouse click makes each bar grow, making them uneven amounts until the set limit is met.


3. Waiting

The input is the count down in words "3... 2... 1..." before the bars change when the mouse is clicked. The change over time is the countdown popping up and disappearing after the bars move.

The unevenness begins when the bars change color as the mouse is clicked, even if it's only clicked once. for example, the bar would begin red and change to green when the mouse is clicked.
